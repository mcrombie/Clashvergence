Victor's History

The Saklian Tribe’s victory was not a matter of holding the widest reach, but of keeping the treasury where it mattered and letting the map answer to that discipline. They began modestly, remained strongest when the board was still contested, and recovered the treasury lead in the middle turns even as rivals briefly surged ahead in territory. Aruangos and Veneunar each showed they could gather regions, but neither converted that breadth into a durable finish; the late gains of Aruangos came too late to dislodge a steadier course. In the end, Saklian’s adaptive coastal method proved the sounder judgment: fewer regions, more coherence, and the first place that the standings ultimately reward.
