Victor's History

The Aruangos Tribe prevailed not by chasing the widest map share, but by keeping its hill-forest discipline intact and turning early consolidation into lasting treasury strength. While others pressed borders or accumulated regions, Aruangos built steadily and created the economic edge that decided both the midgame and the close. The Saklian Watch Rebels showed real reach with 7 regions, yet their advance never matched the winner’s balance of investment and restraint, and they finished well behind on treasury. In the end, the result favored coherence over mere expansion: Aruangos held to its geography, spent with judgment, and finished first where it mattered most.
